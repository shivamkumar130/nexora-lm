Limitations & Future Work
Frame-level classification loses temporal context — consider temporal
models or sliding-window smoothing.
Consider switching to detection + tracking for object-level cues
(hands, items) and behavior heuristics.
Improve dataset size and balance; add augmentation and
cross-camera data.
Add evaluation scripts to compute precision/recall and confusion
matrices.
