CHAPTER 1: WHAT IS MATHEMATICS?
Definition of Mathematics
Mathematics is the science of numbers, quantities, shapes, patterns, relationships, and logical reasoning. It
provides a universal language used to describe the physical world and solve problems.
Mathematics helps us:
Count objects
Measure quantities
Analyze patterns
Predict outcomes
Model real-world situations
Example
If a shop sells one notebook for ₹50 and you buy 5 notebooks:
Cost = 50 × 5 = ₹250
This simple calculation uses mathematics.
