Session 2 Notes: Cofactors, Cramer’s Rule, Determinants and Parabola Topic 1: Cofactors and Minors Definition of Minor: The minor Mij of an element aij is the determinant obtained by deleting the ith row and jth column. Definition of Cofactor: Cij = (-1)^(i+j) Mij Key Idea: Cofactors follow the checkerboard sign pattern: + - + - + - + - + The notebook examples evaluate minors and cofactors directly and verify their values. Topic 2: Adjoint Matrix The adjoint of a matrix is the transpose of the cofactor matrix. Steps: 1. Find all cofactors. 2. Form the cofactor matrix. 3. Take transpose.
