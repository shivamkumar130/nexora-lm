Circle - Complete Master Notes Director Circle Locus of intersection point of perpendicular tangents. For a circle, director circle is concentric with radius = √2 × original radius. Equation: S−S' = 0 Properties of Radical Axis 1. For intersecting circles, common chord = radical axis. 2. For touching circles, common tangent at contact point = radical axis. 3. If two circles cut a third circle orthogonally, their radical axis passes through centre of third circle. Radical Centre Radical axes of three circles intersect at one point called radical centre.
