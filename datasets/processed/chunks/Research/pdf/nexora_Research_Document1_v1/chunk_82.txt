Technology also influences cultural experiences. The widespread use of digital entertainment platforms has changed how people consume media and participate in cultural activities. The relationship between technology and societal norms is bidirectional. While technology challenges and redefines societal norms, societal norms also influence technological development. For instance, growing concerns over data privacy and misinformation on social media platforms have led to policy changes and shifts in the operations of tech companies.
