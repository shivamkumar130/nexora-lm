The numerous ways that people use digital technology are often disregarded by policymakers and the general public, as they are seen as "generic activities" that do not have any specific impact. Given this, it is crucial to acknowledge the complex nature of the effects that digital technology has on adolescents' mental health. This empirical uncertainty is how technology is used.
