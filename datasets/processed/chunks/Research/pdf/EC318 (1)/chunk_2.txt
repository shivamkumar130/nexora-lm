INTRODUCTION Respiration is a very important signal that indicates several health conditions of a human being [1], [2]. If breathing is different from normal, it can cause various health problems, such as stress, Anxiety, sleep-related diseases [3], [4], and lung-related problems. For example, when we breathe fast, it shows stress or panic [3], [5]. And when we release air while exhaling, respiratory issues show based on the temperature of the air [6], [7]. Therefore, we should keep a constant check on it to avoid anxiety and stress [3], [8]. In the past several years, due to the ways of living our lives and problems occurring at the social level, many health-related issues have started happening, like sleep-related disorders [9].The most popular disorder among these is OSA (Obstructive Sleep Apnea) [9], [10]. When we are in sleep mode, it affects the breathing pattern [11]. if this condition is occurs then it affect the human health condition such as heart disease, high blood pressure, and sometimes even sudden death if it is not identified in time [4]. When we are asleep, there is a continuous lack of oxygen, which can cause significant harm to the brain and heart [12]. Therefore, breathing should be continuously and accurately monitored, as it is very important for our daily healthy life. Different sensing methods are used to monitor respiration patterns, like temperature sensors and strain-based sensors [13], [14].
