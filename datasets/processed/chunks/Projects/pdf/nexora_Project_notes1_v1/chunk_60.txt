Project Overview: The Lung Cancer Survival Prediction project was initiated to support oncologists in prognosis prediction and treatment planning. The objective was to build a patients based on clinical and demographic data. The project followed a structured approach: o Data collection and understanding of medical features o Data preprocessing and feature engineering o Model training and evaluation o Analysis of prognostic factors o Deployment planning for clinical applications including: o Impact of cancer stage on survival Effect of different treatment types Demographic factors affecting prognosis o Comorbidities and their impact on outcomes 3. Data Description: The dataset contains clinical information about lung cancer patients and their. survival outcomes. Key Features: o age: Age at diagnosis gender: Patient's gender cancer stage: Stage of cancer at diagnosis (I-IV) family_history: Family history of cancer (Yes/No) smoking_status: Smoking status (Current, Former, Never, Passive). bmi: Body Mass Index - cholesterol Ievel: Cholesterol level. hypertension: Presence of hypertension (Yes/No) asthma: Presence of asthma (Yes/No) cirrhosis: Presence of cirrhosis (Yes/No) other cancer: History of other cancers (Yes/No) treatment_type: Type of treatment received (Surgery, Chemotherapy, Radiation, Combined) treatment_duration: Duration of treatment in days survived: Survival outcome (0 = No, 1 = Yes).
