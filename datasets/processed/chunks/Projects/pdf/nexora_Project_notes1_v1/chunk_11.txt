Today I am presenting our work on Magneto-Inductive Communications for. Littoral Environments.. Traditional underwater communication, like acoustics and radio waves, performs poorly in. shallow water because of multipath, noise, and high attenuation. Magneto-Inductive, or Ml communication, provides an alternative by using stable, low-frequency magnetic fields that can pass through water, sand, and sediment with much less disturbance 2. MI Propagation Principles MI communication works on the idea of a magnetic dipole created by a coil.. Because these magnetic fields are quasi-static, they don't create multipath reflections like. acoustic waves. This gives MI systems predictable behavior, low attenuation, and even covert operation because the electric radiation is minimal.. 3.
