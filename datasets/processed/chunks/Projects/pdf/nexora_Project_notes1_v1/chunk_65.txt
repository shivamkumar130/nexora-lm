Halim. .Novanto Yudistira Candra Dewi Published IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems, Vol. 11, No. 6, December 2024 Abstract The study addresses the issue faced by MSMEs in Indonesia during the pandemic, where erratic commodity prices severely affected their operations. A deep learning model based on Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) is proposed to predict commodity prices and help MSMEs make informed decisions. The model used multivariate time series data collected from PIHPS Nasional and includes features like rice, chicken meat, eggs, and several types of chilies. Key Achievements:. Best MAE: 255.998 Orbit Propagation Module Algorithm: SGP4 (Simple General Perturbation 4) and SDP4 for deep space Libraries: Python sgp4, astropy, skyfield Output: Earth-Centered Inertial (ECl) coordinates (x, y, z in km) and velocity (vx, vy, vz. in km/s) Performance: Can propagate 10,000 satellites in under 1 second Conjunction Engine Module. Two-Stage Filtering: 1. Coarse filter: Distance threshold 10 km (eliminates 99% of pairs, O(n). complexity) 2. Precise filter: Relative velocity >5 km/s, altitude difference <50 km. Min Separation Calculation: Numerical integration using Runge-Kutta 4 method with gradient-based optimization. Collision Probability: Monte Carlo with 1,000 samples, 3o covariance ellipses. Risk LeveIs: LOW (<0.1), MEDIUM (0.1-0.3), HIGH (0.3-0.7), CRITICAL (>0.7) Uncertainty Quantification Module. . Monte Carlo Pipeline: 1. Sample TLE uncertainty: Bstar drag term 5%, mean motion +0.0001 rev/day, inclination 0.001 2. Propagate 1,000 samples using SGP4 for each satellite 3. Compute covariance matrix (33) from position samples 4. Extract 3o ellipse radii from eigenvalues 5. Collision if distance between means < combined 3o radius Output: Mean position, covariance matrix, ellipse radii, collision probability. Layer 3: ML Layer (Detailed).
