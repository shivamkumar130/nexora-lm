Out of Scope (Future Work) Full physics-based simulation Highly dynamic object tracking Large-scale multi-user collaboration 13. Conclusion This project demonstrates how integrating Knowledge Graph reasoning with real-time 3D point cloud visualization inside VR/AR environments significantly enhances digital twin systems. By unifying semantic intelligence and immersive interaction, the proposed platform enables more effective monitoring, analysis, and decision-making in complex real-world systems. 18. One-Line Summary An immersive, intelligent digital twin platform that integrates knowledge graph reasoning. with real-time 3D point cloud visualization for intuitive VR/AR-based interaction and decision support.
