Introduction:. Magneto-Inductive Communications exploit quasi-static magnetic fields generated by coil antennas to transmit information through otherwise challenging media such as water, soil, rock, and industrial environments. This approach offers robust communication where electromagnetic and acoustic signals are strongly attenuated or fail.
