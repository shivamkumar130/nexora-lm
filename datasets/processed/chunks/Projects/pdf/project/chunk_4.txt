VII. DISCUSSION The multivariate LSTM with Adam and BatchNorm achieved the best short-term (1–10 day) prediction accuracy. Attention and Conv-LSTM variants further enhanced learn- ing of cross-commodity dependencies. Batch normalization consistently improved stability and convergence. For long- horizon forecasting (e.g., 30 days), error accumulation remains a limitation; future work may use seq2seq or Transformer- based architectures. VIII. CONCLUSION This study successfully developed and evaluated multiple LSTM-based architectures for forecasting commodity prices across 11 essential commodities in India.
