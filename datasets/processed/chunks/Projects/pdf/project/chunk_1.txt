X, NOVEMBER 2025 1 Multicommodity Price Prediction using LSTM Variants Shivam Kumar (202351130), Dev Saraswat (202351173), Tammineni Harika (202351145) Abstract—This report presents a deep learning approach for predicting commodity prices in India during the COVID-19 pandemic using Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks. The study addresses the challenges faced by Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) due to fluctuating commodity prices. A multivariate LSTM model with the Adam optimizer and Batch Normalization demonstrates superior performance, achieving low Mean Absolute Error (MAE) while reducing overfitting and underfitting risks. We compare several LSTM variants and hybrid architectures, provide implementation de- tails, hyperparameter choices, and reproducible artifacts.
