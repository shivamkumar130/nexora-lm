Discussion, Limitations & Future Work Key points •Frame-only classification misses short-duration gestures — add temporal models. •Fine-tuning corrected baseline class bias and produced balanced predictions. •Lightweight (6.2M params) suitable for edge deployment; temporal enrichment recommended. Future enhancements •Temporal models (LSTM / Transformer) for sequence reasoning. •Detection + tracking (YOLOv8 + DeepSORT) to localize hands/objects. •Dataset expansion (>1000 videos) and ONNX/TensorRT edge optimization. Conclusion & Acknowledgment Conclusion Fine-tuning YOLOv8 classification on curated surveillance frames improved frame-level accuracy from 48.65% to 85.95% (+37.30 pp).
