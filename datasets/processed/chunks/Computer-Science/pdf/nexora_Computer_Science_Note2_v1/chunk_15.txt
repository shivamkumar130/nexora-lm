Ideal Multiple Access Protocol:. o Fair for all nodes Avoids idle time Simple and decentralized Efficient utilization of bandwidth. 1.2 Types of Multiple Access Protocols 1. Channel Partitioning Protocols: TDmA (Time Division Multiple Access): Each node transmits in its own time. slot. FDMA (Frequency Division Multiple Access): Each node has a dedicated frequency band. Random Access Protocols: Pure ALOHA: Transmit anytime; vulnerable time = 2 x frame time; efficiency = 18.4%.
