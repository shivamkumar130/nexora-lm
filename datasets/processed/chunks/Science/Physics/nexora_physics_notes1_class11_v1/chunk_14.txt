Since every measured quantity contains some uncertainty, the resulting quantity also contains uncertainty. The study of how errors combine during mathematical operations is called Propagation of Errors. This chapter covers: Error in Addition Error in Subtraction Error in Multiplication Error in Division Error in Powers General Error Formula Applications Solved Numericals 1. Propagation of Errors When measured quantities are combined mathematically, their errors also combine. Example: Length = (10 ± 0.1) cm Breadth = (5 ± 0.1) cm Area = Length × Breadth The area will also contain error. 2.
